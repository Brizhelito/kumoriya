- **watch-party-realtime**: A Cloudflare Worker acting as the entry point for WebSocket upgrades and internal registry APIs. It delegates stateful room management to two Durable Objects: `PartyRegistryDO` (singleton, managing room creation, invite codes, and user-to-room mappings) and `PartyRoomDO` (per-room instance, managing membership, playback synchronization, and host authority). Authentication is handled via Ed25519-signed JWT session tokens verified at the worker edge before upgrading WebSockets.
- **join-worker**: A lightweight Cloudflare Worker serving as a universal link router for mobile app deep-linking. It validates invite codes, serves Android App Links (`assetlinks.json`), and renders a landing page with intent-based redirects to the native application or fallback download URLs.
