Kumoriya project defaults:

- Be pragmatic with strict boundaries.
- Do not silently expand scope.
- Prefer no match over false match.
- Prefer no stream over wrong stream.
- Use vertical slices.
- Keep plugin contracts explicit.
- Validate with format, analyze, and relevant tests.
- Be honest when a flow was not actually exercised.
- Use Git checkpoints for meaningful phases.
- Favor reviewable diffs over giant edits.
