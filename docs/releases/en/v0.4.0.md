# Kumoriya v0.4.0 — Alpha Release Notes

**Date:** 2026-05-03

## Summary

This release brings the full manga experience to Kumoriya: you can switch between Anime and Manga, read chapters more comfortably, keep your progress, download for offline use, and discover more series from multiple Spanish-language sources.

## Added

- **Manga mode** — you can now switch between Anime and Manga from the main app, and each universe has its own feel.
- **Manga reader** — a new reading experience with vertical and paged modes, chapter navigation, and CBZ support.
- **Manga library** — History, Favorites, and Subscribed now live in a dedicated manga library.
- **More ways to discover manga** — Kumoriya now brings together MangaDex and several Spanish-language sources to help you find more series.
- **Better source and scanlator picking** — choosing what to read is now easier and more accurate.
- **Manga downloads and synced progress** — you can download chapters for offline reading and keep your progress in sync with your account.
- **Better fallback when AniList is down** — the app now shows useful content instead of going blank.
- **Release notifications** — Kumoriya can now be notified when a new release is published.
- **HLS to MP4 support** — HLS video downloads are converted into a more practical format for saving and playback.

## Changed

- **The app styling follows the selected universe** — Anime and Manga now feel visually distinct when you switch.
- **Manga home loads more smoothly** — the manga landing page is now more reliable and quicker to appear.

## Fixed

- **Screen dimming during playback** — the player now keeps the screen awake while a video is playing.
- **Brightness not returning after playback** — leaving the player now restores your previous brightness level.
- **Missed airing notifications** — the app now catches recently aired episodes more reliably.

## Platforms

- Android (arm64-v8a APK)
- Windows (published separately)
